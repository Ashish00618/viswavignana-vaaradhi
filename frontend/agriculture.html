Mission Chetana

Healing Minds, Rebuilding Lives. Our mission to provide compassionate support for mental wellness and a path away from addiction.

Our Path to Mental Wellness

We recognize that mental health is a critical, yet often neglected, aspect of well-being. Mission Chetana aims to destigmatize mental health issues and provide a safe, supportive environment for healing and recovery.

Mental Health Awareness

We conduct workshops and awareness campaigns in communities and schools to break the stigma surrounding mental illness and encourage open conversations.

Confidential Counseling

We offer free and confidential counseling services and support groups, connecting individuals with professionals to help them navigate their challenges.

De-addiction & Rehabilitation

We provide guidance and a strong support system for individuals and families affected by drug addiction, focusing on rehabilitation and relapse prevention.
