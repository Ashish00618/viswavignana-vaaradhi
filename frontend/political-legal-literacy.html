Nyaya Sadan

Justice for All, Not Just for a Few. Our mission to provide free, immediate, and compassionate legal aid to those who need it most.

How We Provide Justice

Nyaya Sadan is dedicated to breaking down the barriers to justice. We offer a range of free services to ensure that no one is denied their rights due to a lack of resources or knowledge.

Free Legal Aid & Representation

We provide qualified legal professionals to represent underprivileged individuals in court, free of charge.

Immediate Assistance

We offer rapid support in police cases, court proceedings, and other urgent legal matters to protect your rights from day one.

Guidance & Awareness

We conduct legal literacy camps and workshops to educate communities about their fundamental rights and legal protections.

Comprehensive Support

Our team provides support for a wide range of legal concerns, ensuring you have guidance at every step of your legal journey.

Our Commitment to the Vulnerable

Nyaya Sadan stands with the most vulnerable members of our society. We are committed to providing dedicated legal support to:

The Poor & Economically Weaker

Ensuring that poverty is not a barrier to accessing justice.

Women & Children

Offering specialized legal protection and support for women and minors facing injustice.

Persons with Disabilities

Advocating for the rights and dignity of individuals with disabilities.

Undertrial Prisoners & Persons in Custody

Providing crucial legal aid to undertrials and those in custody to ensure fair legal proceedings and prevent unjust incarceration.
